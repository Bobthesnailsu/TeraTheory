    Class Changes 11

    Archer Revamp

  • Velik's Mark: The skill will now work for all Archers in the party. Increased damage by 20% will be lowered to 10%.
  • Chase: A new skill (name placeholder) added that allows you to dash up to 16 meters or until you hit a collusion. If you use this while pressing a directional button, you will dash in that direction. Skill learn at level 32. MP costs 188. 25 seconds cooldown.
  • Find Weakness: A new skill added that gives a buff for 8 seconds where the next attack will count as hitting monsters from behind regardless of your positioning. Skill learn at level 40. MP costs 200. 15 seconds cooldown.
  • Rain of Arrows: The first hit that lands on your target will always hit in that specific spot for the remaining hits regardless if the target or yourself are turning. Base damage increased by 10%. II version increased from 8956 to 9852. New visuals.
  • Penetrating Arrow: Base damage increased by 25%. XII version increased from 3849 to 4809.
  • Radiant Arrow: Base damage increased by 25%. IX version increased from 5885 to 7357. Glyph of Carving (rare) changed from double to triple crit chance. Glyph point reduced from 4 to 3.
  • Rapid Fire: Gain 1 stack of focus for each successful hit. Total base damage increased by 37%. BEFORE: Rapid Fire IX Base DMG - 406, 269, 269, 269, 269, 269, 1110. AFTER: Rapid Fire IX Base DMG - 1219, 296, 296, 296, 296, 296, 1221. Glyph of Persistence changed from 10% to 15% chance to reset cooldown. Glyph of Power lowered from 50% to 20%.
  • Arrow Focus: Arrow will continue to receive 1 Arrow Focus per hit. Rapid Fire and Final Shot added to gain Arrow Focus. Upon earning 10 stacks of Arrow Focus, you'll earn a buff for the entire 10 seconds to increase charging speed of any charging skills.
  • Thunderbolt: Base damage increased by 100%. Increased from 5355 to 10711. 2 Noctenium Infusions will be consumed. Noctenium damage bonus increased to 9~13%. New visuals added. Glyph of Carving added to increase crit chance by two. Uncommon master glyph - 4 points. Glyph of Carving added to increase crit chance by three. Rare master glyph - 3 points. Increased damage against monsters with lower HP has been removed to make it deal damage based on the tooltip's base damage now. PvP damage is lowered by 30%.
  • Explosion Trap: Base damage increased by 20%. II version increased from 3190 to 3828.
  • Sniper's Eye: Effect changed from "Increased damage to boss monsters by 5%" to "Increased damage to all monsters by 7%".
  • Eagle's Eye: Stance is removed.
  • Poison Arrow: Casting time has been removed. Poison stacks can go up to three times.
  • Sequential Fire: Final Salvo will be replaced for this new skill with different functionality and different skill icon. Reduced the amount of shots from 5 to 1. Base damage increased by 40% damage where base damage becomes 1177. Gain 3 stacks of Focus when landing a successful hit. Glyph of Persistence has been removed. Glyph of Energy added to reduce cooldown by 25%. Common glyph - 4 points. Glyph of Energy added to reduce cooldown by 35%. Uncommon master glyph - 3 points.
  • Backstep/Breakaway Bolt: If you stand still when using these skills, you will move backwards, towards your camera. If you use these skills when pressing a directional button, the skill will be cast in the direction you are moving.
  • Snare: Glyph of Enfeeblement will be removed from this skill. It has become a two-part skill. Shoot an arrow up to 22m and press the same skill again to explode that deals AoE damage and slows the target's movement speed for 3 seconds. Lowers movement speed by 90% against any monsters unless immune. Lowers movement speed by 20% against players. New skill display, functionality, and sound. Casting time has been removed. You first learn this skill at level 30. Cooldown increased from 15 to 20 seconds. MP cost changed from 250 to 380. Base damage on Ensnaring Trap II changed from 449 to 625, 833.
  • Crack Shot: Crit chance passive effect lowered from 15% to 10%.
