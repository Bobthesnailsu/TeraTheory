Fury

"Fury", is an inherent skill property that increases the skill damage based on the user's current HP.

The skills with this property are as follows:

  • Berserker: Lethal Strike, Vampiric Blow
  • Warrior: Rising Fury
  • Slayer: Fury Strike

    • For the Berserker skills (Lethal Strike, Vampiric Blow) and Warrior's Rising Fury, the damage is determined by:
